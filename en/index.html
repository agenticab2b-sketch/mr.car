/// Car repair in Tallinn

Professional car repair and maintenance in Tallinn

Mr.Car is a reliable car service in Tallinn, located in the Kopli area — just a few minutes from the city center. Our experienced mechanics offer a wide range of services: general car repair, computer diagnostics, tire works and replacement, suspension and brake repair, engine overhaul, gearbox repair, air conditioning service, and much more.

We use modern diagnostic equipment and high-quality spare parts from well-known manufacturers. Every repair begins with a thorough inspection — we identify the problem, provide an accurate quote, and agree on deadlines before starting the work. We provide a guarantee for all performed work.

The history of cars previously serviced with us is saved in a database, allowing us to timely remind you of the need for the next maintenance. This helps prevent unexpected expensive repairs and keeps your car in top condition at all times.

Mr.Car auto repair — fair price, fast service, and a professional approach. Book an appointment by calling +372 5646 1210 or leave a request online.

/// Frequently asked questions

Questions and answers

How long does car repair take at Mr.Car?

Most maintenance and repair work is completed on the same day. More complex jobs can take 2–5 business days. We confirm the exact timing after diagnostics.

Does Mr.Car provide a warranty on completed work?

Yes, we provide a warranty on all completed work. We use only quality original parts or proven alternatives. Warranty terms depend on the type of work performed.

How much does car repair cost at Mr.Car?

Oil change starts from 35€, diagnostics from 40€, and tire services from 20€ per wheel. We provide an exact quote after inspecting the car. Labour starts from 45€ per hour. See the pricing section for more details.

Where is Mr.Car auto service located?

We are located in Tallinn at Kopli 82a, just a few minutes from the city centre. We are open Monday to Friday from 09:00 to 18:00. Open the map.

Which car brands does Mr.Car repair?

We repair all car brands, including Volkswagen, Audi, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Toyota, Volvo, Ford, Skoda, Peugeot, Renault and many others.
