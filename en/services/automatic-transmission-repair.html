Specialized Automatic Transmission Diagnostics & Repair in Tallinn

An automatic transmission is one of the most complex assemblies in a modern car. It contains hundreds of hydraulic, mechanical, and electronic components operating under high pressure with high precision. A failure in one part can quickly spread to the entire system.

Mr.Car specializes in automatic transmission diagnostics and repair. We use dealer-level equipment to pinpoint the exact cause of the malfunction and prevent expensive complete replacement. We repair classic automatics, as well as DSG and ZF gearboxes.

Why shouldn't you delay addressing transmission problems?
Automatic transmission failures won't fix themselves — damage worsens with every mile driven. Metal debris spreads through the system, clogging valve body channels and destroying hydraulics. The earlier you seek help, the lower the repair scope and cost. Delaying often leads to full transmission replacement.

Main symptoms of automatic transmission failure

Jerking and Clunking
Strong jerks or clunks during shifting – especially when accelerating or braking. This indicates hydraulic system or mechatronics issues.
Slipping Gears
Pöörded tõusevad, aga auto ei võta kiirust üles. Siduripaketid libisevad ja jõud ei jõua ratasteni.
Viivitused (uimane lülitamine)
Auto "mõtleb" enne käigu sisselülitamist või jääb käikude vahele paus – käiguvahetus on ebaloomulikult aeglane.
Müra ja vilin
Sõidu ajal või kiirendades on kuulda ebatavalist vilinat, undamist või metalsest kolinat.
Õlilekked
Auto all on näha tumedaid või punakaid õlilaike. Õlitaseme langus põhjustab käigukasti ülikiire kulumise.
Limp Mode
Armatuuril süttib veateade ja auto lülitub avariirežiimi, et vältida suuremaid kahjustusi. Vajalik on kohene diagnostika.

What happens if you ignore an automatic transmission failure?

1
First Symptoms

Minor delays, light jerking — diagnostics reveal the issue at an early stage. An oil change or valve body cleaning is often enough.

2
Damage Worsens

Transmission slips, limp mode activates. Metal shards spread through the system. Valve body and torque converter repairs are needed.

3
Complete Lockup

The transmission refuses to shift gears. The entire assembly must be removed and rebuilt or replaced. The most expensive scenario.

Automatic Transmission Diagnostics — The Only Right First Step

Successful transmission repair always starts with accurate diagnostics. Blind part replacement wastes money and rarely solves the root cause.

  • Computer Diagnostics — reading fault codes and live data
  • Hydraulic Pressure Measurement — checking valve body functionality
  • Oil Analysis — detecting metal shavings and signs of wear

Main Automatic Transmission Repair Solutions

Based on diagnostic results, we select the most appropriate path

1. Oil and Filter Change

Dynamic (flush) oil change and system cleaning. Removes wear deposits from old oil and fixes light jerking in early stages.

2. Mechatronics Repair

Valve body washing, replacing defective solenoids and seals. Restores fast shifting, removes delays, and eliminates pressure loss.

3. Complete Overhaul

Removing and dismantling the transmission. Replacing clutch packs, seals, and damaged parts. Required when severe mechanical destruction occurs.

DSG and ZF Transmissions — High-Tech Solutions

DSG (Direct Shift Gearbox) is a dual-clutch transmission developed by Volkswagen Group, found in VW, Audi, Škoda, and SEAT vehicles. DSG is sensitive to oil cleanliness and mechatronic faults: typical failures involve the clutch pack and the mechatronics unit. Mr.Car repairs DSG gearboxes using special tools.

ZF (ZF Friedrichshafen) produces widely used automatic transmissions (8HP, 9HP) for premium vehicles like BMW, Volvo, Land Rover, and others. ZF transmissions require specialized diagnostic tools and specific viscosity transmission fluid. We provide qualified diagnostics and repair for ZF gearboxes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a classic automatic and a DSG?
A classic automatic (hydromechanical) uses a torque converter for smooth power delivery. DSG is a dual-clutch transmission. It shifts significantly faster and more efficiently, but is built differently and requires a specific maintenance approach.
What is the difference between an automatic transmission and a CVT?
A CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission) feels like a regular automatic, but it does not have stepped gears. Mr.Car specializes only in stepped automatic and robotized transmissions (AT, DSG, ZF). We do not repair CVTs.
How much does automatic transmission repair cost?
Since an automatic transmission is a highly complex unit, the detailed repair cost only becomes clear after diagnostics. Computer scanning, pressure checks, and test drives reveal exactly what needs restoring or replacing. We provide a clear estimate before work starts.
Can you drive a car in limp mode?
Limp Mode on kaitsefunktsioon, mis pannakse peale kasti eluga päästmiseks. Autoga avariirežiimis sõita ei tohi! Sõiduk tuleb toimetada koheselt töökotta diagnostikale.
How often should you change automatic transmission oil?
Changing the automatic transmission fluid and filter is recommended every 60,000 – 80,000 km to ensure a long lifespan and smooth operation.
What happens if you delay a transmission oil change too long?
Old oil loses its lubricating properties and gathers metal dust from wearing friction plates. This acts as an abrasive, wearing down valve body valves, seals, and filters. This leads to pressure drop and transmission death.
