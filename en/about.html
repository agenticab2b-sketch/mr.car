Your Reliable Partner

Learn more about our company and work process. Mr.Car is designed to provide the best service.

Mr.Car auto service in Tallinn is a modern and reliable car service, officially operating under the company Anet Eesti OÜ. We provide maintenance and repair for passenger cars and vans, helping car owners in Tallinn and Harju County keep their vehicles safe, reliable, and technically sound all year round.

Our goal is to provide a car service where the customer can be confident in both the quality of the work and clear communication.

Well-thought-out and Professional Work Process

At Mr.Car auto service in Tallinn, every job is based on a clear and professional process. Maintenance and repair work is carried out in accordance with car manufacturers' technical requirements and documentation.

We strictly follow repair technology and the correct sequence of work so that the result is durable and the car works as intended after the repair. We do not use temporary or simplified solutions if they may affect the vehicle's safety, reliability, or further operation.

Car Service in Tallinn

Mr.Car auto service offers a wide range of car repair and maintenance services:

  • Routine car maintenance and technical inspection
  • Engine repair
  • Brake system repair
  • Suspension repair
  • Gearbox maintenance and repair
  • Exhaust system and muffler repair and manufacturing
  • Welding works
  • Wheel alignment
  • Air conditioning and climate system maintenance and filling

In addition, we perform:

  • Computer diagnostics
  • Coding of electronic systems
  • Installation and maintenance of Webasto
  • Installation of remote start systems
  • Various technical upgrades

If desired, we also offer car detailing services so that your vehicle looks as good as it drives.

Clear and Honest Communication

We place great importance on transparent communication. Before starting work, we explain the vehicle's technical condition, discuss the necessary work, and agree on the pricing.

During the repair, we keep the customer informed about the progress of the work, and upon completion, we provide an understandable and simple overview of the work done.

Customer Trust

The quality of our work is confirmed by customer feedback — a 4.9 Google rating based on the experiences of real customers.
